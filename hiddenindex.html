How it works

From a blank slate to a publishable schedule in four steps.

Step 1

Talk to set up

Name your division, roles, services, FTEs, and constraints. The assistant validates and summarizes back to you.

Step 2

Import history

Upload old schedules. We detect patterns and edge-case rules automatically.

Step 3

Generate & review

Create a draft with justifications and fairness tallies. Make edits by chat or directly on the grid.

Step 4

Publish & notify

Push to calendars, email the team, and freeze versions with an audit trail for governance.

Security by design

We follow a defense-in-depth approach aligned with health system best practices.

  • HTTPS everywhere, HSTS, and secure headers
  • Azure AD and SSO-ready authentication
  • Secrets management and least-privilege access
  • Input validation and rate limiting
  • Automated backups and point-in-time restore
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) and audit logs
  • Monitoring, alerting, and anomaly detection
